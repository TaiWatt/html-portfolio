The Best Movies According to Melon

My Top 3 movies of all-time

Toy Story 4

This series is close to my heart but the fourth one especially talks to me about moving on to adulthood and keeping sweet memories in our heart.

The Terminal

Reminds me how we should try to be a genuinely good person and pass the love forward.

Blue Giant

So much raw emotion and hearty jazz music that speaks to our soul to fly.
